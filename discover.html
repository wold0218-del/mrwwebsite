Why I Chose the University of Minnesota Duluth

I chose UMD because of the pretty campus, medium-sized student body, and proximity to home. The community makes me proud to be a Bulldog, and I love the opportunities to get involved on campus. The music program is also fantastic, and I knew it would be a great fit for me.

What I Like About Duluth and the Surrounding Area

I really enjoy Duluth because it has Lake Superior, a variety of things to do outside, and the smaller community feel. I love going on walks along the lake, exploring new trails up north, and taking photos of the area. The changing seasons make living here truly special.

Personal Experiences and Campus Highlights

  • My favorite class is piano ensemble. Playing with other musicians is a great experience.
  • I enjoyed living in the dorms, but am happy to live off-campus as an upperclassman.
  • My favorite spot on campus is Weber Music Hall. Watching performances there is always a highlight.
  • My favorite event is the annual homecoming celebration. The energy and school spirit are infectious!
