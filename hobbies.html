My Hobbies:

Music & Piano

I've been playing piano since I was 7, and it has become a huge part of my identity. One memorable experience was teaching my first piano student during my sophomore year it taught me patience, creativity, and confidence. Music brings me joy and allows me to express myself. I love learning new pieces with the end goal of performing for my friends and family.

Thrifting

Thrifting is a fun way to find unique clothes and accessories while being environmentally friendly. I enjoy the thrill of the hunt and the satisfaction of finding hidden gems. It's also a great way to express my personal style without breaking the bank. I've found some of my favorite pieces at thrift stores!

Exploring Duluth & the Outdoors

Duluth's trails, Lake Superior views, and scenic hikes are some of my favorite ways to relax and recharge. I love going on walks along the lake, exploring new parks, and taking photos of the area. The changing seasons make every visit feel different.

Why These Activities Matter to Me

These hobbies help me stay balanced, creative, and grounded. Music lets me express myself, thrifting allows me to be resourceful and creative, and exploring the outdoors helps me reset. Each one brings joy and adds something meaningful to my everyday life.
